House group is a great place to meet other Christians, be encouraged in our walk with Christ, enjoy friendship and fellowship and learn more about God. It's also a place to have fun, share food and spend time with each other. Come along and join us...

Who's it for?

Young people aged 10-14.

Where does it meet?

We meet at the house of one of the leaders.

When does it meet?

Every other Tuesday during term time, at 7.30 pm.
